Details of the BCA


The department of Computer Applications was started in the year 2002. There was urgent need to take a turn to vocational courses in the wake of employment driven education policy of the union government and similar perceptions echoed by University Grants Commission. We admit 60 students in this course every year. The process of admission was through entrance examination followed by interview till 2015- 2016 session. The Kolhan University adopted the method of Centralized admission from 2016- 2017. As it was a new development and the criterion for admission was also changed (Condition of 45% marks in Mathematics was made compulsory for admission) therefore the number of admissions has dropped temporarily. This department has very successfully catered to the needs of the students not only from the state of Jharkhand but also to students from Bihar, Bengal and Odisha.

Course Structure

  • There are two semesters in an academic year . The entire programme consist of 6 semesters . Each semsters have 5 papers of 100 marks

  • A student shall have to secure minimum 45% marks in both end semsters and internal semesters in both theory and practical papers, seperately to pass

Exam Structure

  • The internal semesters in theory and practical will be of hour duration carrying 30 marks(25 marks of Unit Test and 5 marks for attendance) for each paper.

  • A student has to maintains 75% attendance seperately in every subject to qualify for End-Semesters.

Activites

  • Seminars are organised in the department on regular basis where repute from different institution of Jharkhand are invited. It helps students to in assesing their status in real industry

  • Workshops are a ready solution for our budding computer professional to equip them with latest changes in the tech and services associated to it.
