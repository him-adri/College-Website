Eligibilty Criteria

Eligibilty for Admission

  • A candidate may be admitted to BCA cousre for the degree of Bachelor of Computer Applications , if he or she passed the Intermidiate examinations(I.SC./I.Com/I.A./10+2) of a borad / University established by law or any other examination recognized by the university as eqivalent there to with minimum 45% aggregrate along with Maths as a subject.
  • The candidates filling the criterion can apply for admission in BCA directly on the website or by visting the college campus by purchasing the prospectus and admission form from the college.

Selection Procedure

  • A candidate seeking admission needs to apply for the course on or before the last date; admission form must be filled properly and clearly, supported with scanned documents of marksheet, school/ College leaving Certificate and Migration Certificate of Intermidiate Exams

  • A list of candidates will be announced. The selected candiadtes must take admission by producing all required documents and after paying the fess electronically or by Demand Draft.

  • The college reserves rights to cancel the candidature of any canditate in case of discrepancy. No personal meeting will be tolarated in case of no addmission

Cousre Details


Course Structure

  • There are two semesters in an academic year . The entire programme consist of 6 semesters . Each semsters have 5 papers of 100 marks

  • A student shall have to secure minimum 45% marks in both end semsters and internal semesters in both theory and practical papers, seperately to pass

Exam Structure

  • The internal semesters in theory and practical will be of hour duration carrying 30 marks(25 marks of Unit Test and 5 marks for attendance) for each paper.

  • A student has to maintains 75% attendance seperately in every subject to qualify for End-Semesters.

Activites

  • Seminars are organised in the department on regular basis where repute from different institution of Jharkhand are invited. It helps students to in assesing their status in real industry

  • Workshops are a ready solution for our budding computer professional to equip them with latest changes in the tech and services associated to it.
